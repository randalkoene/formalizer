Formalizer: Help: Hard Time Getting Back into Work

I'm having a hard time getting back into work after a non-work interval (e.g., lunch, dinner).

1 Use active task chunking - keep the daemon on! System Summary: Action#19; SYSTEM USE summary (e.g. in lists.html)
2 Be keenly aware of just how much there is out there that you intend to get done in the next weeks. It can help to gain motivating perspective by paging through the AL CTR. (Note that this exercise, at this point, seems like the opposite of the one that is essential while aiming to focus on doing a Task, namely to exist only in the moment. Actually, the two go hand in hand.) Formalizers: AL CTR
3 Take physical steps to switch mentally 'back in'. For example, take a shower, meditate, put on a type of music, have some coffee (or armodafinil, if it's that sort of day), or have something to calm nerves and help focus/flow (phenibut, even alcohol).
4 Choose a Standing Desk posture if at all possible.
5 Update a bunch of System Metrics (e.g. Time Tracking, Daily, Points Testing), especially any that are used to track Criteria Steps of Active Milestones. System Summary: Activity: All Day: #22 and #23
6 Do this before a quick AL update, because you need this information! Identify remaining targets for the day:
  1. Normal System Metrics - explicitly note which ones lag!
  2. Intentions and Progress hours still needed to achieve good productivity.
  3. DAF and Active Milestone Steps.
  4. Foundational LEARNED-LVL1 target behaviors that can still be accomplished (but on this day have not yet been accomplished).
System Summary: Morning Metrics;
Self Evaluation: Time Tracking;
Decisions & Communications & Challenges: DAF Challenges;
Self Evaluation: Milestone Steps;
LEARNED-LVL1: Day Worksheet
7 Make a very aware and conscious selection of the next task, using overall task selection advice and tasks that work on Criteria Steps of Active Milestones in prioritized proportions. Do a quick AL update as needed to make this selection. System Summary: Task Selection; Personal Roadmap v1.0 suport doc: Recalibrate Active Milestones
8 Remember that you are doing immediate support things for the people you love, and you are doing impact things for the humanity and the world that you love. It's not all about your own little story, and so you don't have to worry about your own respectability.
9 Toss Fear out with a vengeance (possibly even some swearing). TL#202004071558.3
10 Meditate as needed. System Summary: Task Selection
11 OM chanting with deep breathing that can put you into a narrowly focused, lightly sedated and detached state of mind. YouTube: Playlist: Relaxing Zen Music
