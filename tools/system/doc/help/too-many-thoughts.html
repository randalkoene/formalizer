Formalizer: Help: Too Many Thoughts

There are too many thoughts, things to keep in mind, perceived balls to keep in the air. My mind is spinning a bit.

1 Remind yourself that there is s Formalizer System in place that can hold every one of the thoughts or concerns and that will present them as needed. Nothing will be forgotten. Formalizer: AL CTR; DIL Entry; Plan DIL Entry
2 Put on some OM chant music or a similar very simple and peaceful grounding sound. YouTube: Playlist: Relaxing Zen Music
3 Take deep rhythmic breaths. Release tension, in your jaw, in your face, in your shoulders, in your body.
4 If possible, stand and be grounded.
5 Stop your inner monologue. Set aside yesterday and tomorrow. Only this moment exists.
6 If necessary, carry out a full Meditation session.
