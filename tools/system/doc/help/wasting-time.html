Formalizer: Help: Wasting Time

I'm wasting time (e.g. video, news).

1 Set aside, wipe away inner monologue. Only this moment exists. You don't talk to yourself. You do.
2 Ban all video for the rest of the day. When you need mind breaks, go outside or look outside, or exercise.
3 Immediately, turn off all video and audio. Later, you may reinitroduce non-interruptive audio, but no video.
4 Think: What would the bravest person do? Do that.
5 Take a focus chemical. This could be nicotine gum, armodafinil, etc.
