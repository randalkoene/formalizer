Phase Threads

  1. At any point in time, I am in a phase that is characterized by having some subset of priority Milestones. Those Milestones are either in the Ideal Situation set or dependencies thereof. These are presented in the board.

  2. The point of this is to not spread my focus to thinly, to take sensible turns for focus.

  3. A sensible order of activities based on the dependencies hierarchy prioritizes Phase threads in the .

  4. The scheduling order is regularly corrected to align with the dependencies hierarchy by using the Apply TD Changes feature on Node Boards.

  5. The Schedule contains recognized Promises and Challenges. These are intended to be addressed during times of the when my energy and mental state are best set to do so.

Progress Feedback

  1. It is very important to make progress in a way that is immediately measurable and felt. For this, I select threads that can be accomplished in that amount of time.

  2. The Weekly Goals work on the Phase Threads. The Weekly Goals are reviewed and updated each week based on the sensibly updated Schedule (a week long look-ahead and update of upcoming Nodes).

  3. The Weekly Goals threads are split to address 2 categories, presently work and self-work. At times, the split is meant to be 50/50, and self-work can be aimed almost exclusively on one thing, presently my financial situation. The intended split - or time allocation - is expressed explicitly in the .

  4. Start each day with some perspective while using the to guide me. Finalize and review tracked metrics of the preceding day.

  5. Are there realizations? Priorities? New Nodes needed?

  6. During Scheduling, prefer Nodes that appear in Weekly Main Goals. Plan, during this day, to do something externally visible (add NNL here!).

  7. Frequently (re)check the order of VTD/UTD Nodes in the Schedule to ensure that it is sensible.

  8. During the day, keep track of actual progress. At a minimum, check during the second mini-day update. Here, I try to identify the intended distribution:

    • I am spending time during the day in a way that will result in good Day Review scores. (Needs a more flexible script for automation.)
    • I am making measurable progress towards Weekly Main Goals during the day. (Needs before and now measures to compare.)
    • I am balancing progress in 2 categories.
